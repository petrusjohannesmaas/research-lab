Solidity

Solidity is a programming language for implementing smart contracts on various blockchain platforms, most notably, Ethereum.

Solidity is licensed under GNU General Public License v3.0. It was designed by Gavin Wood and developed by Christian Reitwiessner, Alex Beregszaszi, and several former Ethereum core contributors. Programs in Solidity run on Ethereum Virtual Machine or on compatible virtual machines.

Solidity was proposed in August 2014 by Gavin Wood. The language was later developed by the Ethereum project's Solidity team, led by Christian Reitwiessner. It is the primary language used to develop smart contracts for Ethereum as well as other private blockchains, such as the enterprise-oriented Hyperledger Fabric blockchain.
